Most plots are named "type + gridNr".pdf for example burnedall0.pdf

gridNr:
0: Normal grid
1: hex grid
2: triangle grid

type:
burnedall: % burned of all simulations
fracburned: avg % burned
oppreached: % of simulations where the fire reached the opposite
opptime: avg number of ticks to reach the opposite of the grid. Only simulations that actually reached the opposite are counted for.
opptimeall: ticks to reach the oppisite for all simulations where the fire reached the opposite.
firefighter[density]: the amount of alive and dead firefighters for the given density. (Could be an empty plot)
